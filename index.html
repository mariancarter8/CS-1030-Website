Horizon Zero Dawn

Plot

The plot of Horizon Zero Dawn is a story of tragedy and hope. It starts with the main character Aloy being born and growing up as an outcast of her tribe. When she reaches 18, she completes a trial and is let into the tribe. She decides to go on a journey to figure out why she was born and why the machines in her world have started to be aggressive. She finds out the world has ended and humanity was able to restart because of an AI. Aloy was made to help put the world back together and restore the AI.

Gameplay

This game is an open world with a large map that lets the player explore wherever they want. Depending on the player's level will determine if they will have an easier or harder time in certain areas. As they progress through the game they will unlock different weapons and armour for Aloy.

Weapon Name Weapon description
Icerail The icerail freezes the enemy so that Aloy can deal extra damage
Ropecaster The ropecaster ties up enemies so that they become easier to hit
Sharpshot Bow The sharpshot bow weapon takes longer to come to a full draw. The trade-off is that the weapon deals a higher amount of damage.
Spear The spear is the only melee weapon that Aloy has. It allows player to have a variety of light and heavy attacks.
Tripcaster The tripcaster is a trap type of weapon. It can help Aloy be more stealthy and take down multiple enemies.
